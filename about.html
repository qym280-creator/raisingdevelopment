About Us關於我們关于我们

Built at the Intersection of Infrastructure, Capital, and Technology構建於基建、資本與技術的交匯點构建于基建、资本与技术的交汇点

Raising Development is an infrastructure investment and asset management firm dedicated to empowering the future of infrastructure. We combine deep on-the-ground operational expertise with institutional-grade capital markets capabilities: structuring, financing, building, operating, and exiting infrastructure assets across their full life-cycle. By embedding modern methodology, including distributed ledger technology, into how we manage assets and capital, we help institutional investors access high-quality real-world cash flows with greater transparency, efficiency, and trust. The real challenge of modern infrastructure investment is not any single dimension in isolation — not engineering alone, not finance alone, not technology alone — but the systemic integration of all three at the intersection of a new era. Teams with any single dimension are not rare; teams with all three are extremely scarce.現代基礎設施投資的真正挑戰，不在於其中任何一個單獨維度——不在於只懂工程、也不在於只懂金融、也不在於只懂技術——而在於這三者在新時代交匯點上的系統性整合。具備任意單一維度的團隊並不罕見，但同時具備三者的複合創始團隊，極為稀缺。现代基础设施投资的真正挑战，不在于其中任何一个单独维度——不在于只懂工程、也不在于只懂金融、也不在于只懂技术——而在于这三者在新时代交汇点上的系统性整合。具备任意单一维度的团队并不罕见，但同时具备三者的复合创始团队，极为稀缺。
